Relationship Horoscope by Liz Greene

Psychology and astrology can offer many insights into the inner mechanisms of relationships. The combination of both - a psychological view of the partners and their needs, enlightened by the deep wisdom of the horoscope - can help us to find our way through the sometimes uncertain sphere of love more consciously and with more alternatives and choices.
