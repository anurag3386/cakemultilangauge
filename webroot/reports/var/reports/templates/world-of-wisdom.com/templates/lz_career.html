Career and Vocation by Liz Greene

This report explores the tension between this inner vocation and everyday work, between the daily "service" and the existential necessity to do something meaningful. It describes the complex processes which can be triggered by inner and outer duties. It examines your strengths, talents, and personal limitations, together with your attitude towards success as well as working with others.
