Our relationships provide us with some of the deepest and most mysterious experiences of our lives. Why do we feel attracted to some people, but not to others? Why does love fade away in some cases and yet last for decades in others? Why does love mean happiness and fulfilment for some people, while others experience hate and destructiveness?

The 38 - 40 page text is centered around the following topics:
  • What brings you together: The essential energies at work between you and your partner.
  • What the two of you create: Your relationship as an independent entity
  • Your relationship and yourself: Your relationship and your partner.
  • Deeper issues activated inside.

The Relationship Horoscope is designed for love relationships between two adults. It may therefore be of limited use for other kinds of relationships. Liz Greene's language is lively and inspiring, without astrological jargon, but very profound in psychological terms. Her interpretation of your relationship can lead the way towards further personal growth and a more fulfilling relationship.
